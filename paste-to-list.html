Paste to Markdown List

Instructions

  1. Find the text to convert to a Markdown list (e.g., in another browser tab)
  2. Copy it to the clipboard (Ctrl+C, or ⌘+C on Mac)
  3. Paste it into this window (Ctrl+V, or ⌘+V on Mac)
  4. The converted Markdown list will appear!

The conversion is performed locally in your browser.
